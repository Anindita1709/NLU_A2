Indian Institute of Technology Jodhpur was established in 2008 to foster technology education and research in India. The institute emphasizes scholarship in teaching and learning, scholarship in research and creative accomplishments, and relevance to industry. IIT Jodhpur operates from an 852-acre residential permanent campus on National Highway 65 near Jodhpur toward Nagaur. Student life is described as vibrant and holistic, with emphasis on extracurricular activities, community service, and social responsibility.

[About IITJ > Institute > Introduction to IIT Jodhpur]
Indian Institute of Technology Jodhpur was established in 2008 to foster technology education and research in India. The institute is committed to technological thought and action for the country's economic development. It highlights teaching-learning, research and creative accomplishments, and industry relevance as its three driving forces. The campus is an 852-acre residential permanent campus on National Highway 65, planned as a symbol of academics that is simple yet profound. IIT Jodhpur also states that it follows a multidisciplinary approach to technology development, with state-of-the-art laboratories and academic degree programs organized through departments while research activities are coordinated through centers for technologies.

[About IITJ > Institute > Vision and Mission]
Vision: A future-driven institute that nurtures excellence of thought; creates, preserves, and imparts knowledge; and uses transformational technologies and interventions with a multidisciplinary approach to respond to societal challenges and aspirations.

Mission highlights:
- Foster humanitarian values, passion for learning, and creativity in faculty and students.
- Build a high-quality, futuristic educational and research ecosystem.
- Develop socially responsible faculty, students, and future leaders committed to a self-reliant India.
- Catalyze a professional internal culture, enabling infrastructure, and ancillary services.
- Forge national and international collaborations with industry and academia.

Goals mentioned on the site include curriculum breadth and specialization, futuristic pedagogy, globally engaged research with state-of-the-art facilities, outreach for lifelong learning, institutional collaboration, industry connect, innovative financial planning, futuristic infrastructure, a strong student life cycle, and an agile people-centric organization.
